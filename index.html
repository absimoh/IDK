STARS & GALAXIES

Spiral Galaxies

Flat, rotating disk containing stars, gas and dust, and a central concentration of stars known as the bulge.

Nebulae

Giant clouds of dust and gas in space. Sometimes they are the nurseries where new stars are born.

Black Holes

A place in space where gravity pulls so much that nothing can get out, not even light.

KNOWLEDGE BASE

Why is Pluto not a planet anymore? +
Pluto is now classified as a dwarf planet because it does not meet all three criteria to be a full planet (specifically clearing its orbit).
What is the Great Red Spot? +
It is a giant storm that has raged on Jupiter for at least 350 years. It is so large that Earth could fit inside it.
